About Us

BANGKU is a restaurant dedicated to bringing you the most authentic Filipino foods. Our store owner and chef, Rosslax, has invented many of the delicious dishes we offer, ensuring a truly unique and authentic dining experience.

Rosslax's passion for Filipino cuisine shines through in every dish, making BANGKU a must-visit destination for food lovers.
